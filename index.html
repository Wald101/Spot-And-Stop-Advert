The Problem

South Africa is a beautiful country. Filled with great people, great cities and beautiful nature. Although South Africa is a great country, crime, poverty and corruption is hindering South Africa's growth. Crime is an ever increasing problem in South Africa.

Most crime is not even reported to the police due to outdated and unintuitive crime reporting processes. Due to this crime hotspots are misrepresented. Due to this emergency services can't be as effective as they should be.

Meet Spot-And-Stop

Spot-And-Stop is a mobile application which give people the power to fight crime and stay safe. Using the technology available in modern smartphones, Spot-And-Stop makes reporting crime safe and easy. Using the GPS is a smartphone along with software and hardware controls, Spot-And-Stop enables users to report different crimes at or near their current location either by using the application user interface or using combinations of button presses on their devices.

Spot-And-Stop not only allows people to report crime, it also notifies the police of crime once it is reported. Since police will be notified of crime as it happens and can respond accordingly. Spot-And-Stop will not only notify police but also tell them what sort of crime is being commited. Spot-And-Stop also notifies users when they are near crime hotspots so that they can safely go about their daily business.
